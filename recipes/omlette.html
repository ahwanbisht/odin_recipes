Egg Omlette Recipe

It is the best breakfast for a student like me. Full of nutrition fill your stomach well upto noon and one tray of eggs can make your breakfast for upto 3 weeks.

It is also very easy to make, easiest in my opinion to think that a dummy like me can make it eyes closed. Only takes 5 mins but also make a roti aur paratha and chai with it to get the work done in your stomach and put it on lockdown

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Onion- 1 finely chopped medium sized onion
  • Green Chilli - 1 finely chopped
  • Turmeric/dhania powder - 1 tbsp
  • red chilli powder- 1 tbsp
  • salt - 1 tbsp
  • (Not Necessary)people also add milk to the omlette as it helps fluffy it up.

Steps (Instructions)

  1. Take onion, green chilli, turmeric, salt and pepper in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Crack open couple of eggs in that and mix well.
  3. Heat a tawa and drizzle in some oil.
  4. Once it is hot pour the egg mixture in it and cook on low to medium heat till the top layer sets, It will take around 5 mins.
  5. Now flip over and cook for 5 mins.
  6. Remove it carefully and serve with partha and chai.
